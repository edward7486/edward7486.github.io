About

I’m Edward Nguyen, currently a UX Designer at Costco IT where I’m helping shape the user experiences for the technical future at Costco. I previously led design at Skilljar and started my career at LiquidPlanner. As a student, I interned with teams at Apptio and Vertafore.

I’m passionate about designing products or services that improve the quality of life for people. To do so, I believe great design comes from deeply understanding problems and following a defined but fluid user-centric process to arrive at a solution.
